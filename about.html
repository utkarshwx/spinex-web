About Spinex

Spinex is Odisha’s leading interventional pain clinic, specializing in treating a comprehensive range of chronic pain conditions. Whether you’re struggling with back pain, debilitating neuropathy, or lingering sports injuries, our multidisciplinary team utilizes personalized care plans and cutting-edge techniques to help you reclaim mobility, comfort, and a life free from pain. Spinex is here to partner with you on your journey towards a healthier, pain-free future.


About the Director

Dr. Pritam Mohanty, the Director of Spinex, is a skilled, friendly & energetic doctor with a passion for helping people overcome pain by making use of non-invasive medical interventions. With a background in pain management, Dr. Mohanty has gained a reputation for his innovative treatment methods and compassionate care.
His approach to pain management combines cutting-edge technology with a holistic understanding of the human body. Dr. Mohanty believes in treating the root cause of pain, not just the symptoms, to ensure lasting relief. Under his leadership, Spinex envisions to become the most trusted name in Bhubaneswar for effective and empathetic pain management.
