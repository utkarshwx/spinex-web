Other Treatment Options





These options explore innovative and complementary approaches for pain management. We believe in offering a holistic treatment plan, and these options can be used alongside traditional methods to enhance your pain relief journey.

• Morphine Pumps


Implanted devices deliver pain medication directly into the bloodstream for long-term pain control. This can be an option for those with severe chronic pain who require continuous pain management.

• Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) and Chondrocyte Implantation


These techniques utilize your own cells or healthy cartilage cells to promote healing in damaged joints. PRP injections can help stimulate tissue regeneration and reduce pain in conditions like arthritis.

• Stem Cell Therapy


An emerging treatment using stem cells to promote tissue regeneration and pain relief. While still under research, stem cell therapy holds promise for treating various chronic pain conditions.

• Ozone Discectomy


This minimally invasive procedure uses ozone gas to treat herniated discs. Ozone gas can help shrink the disc and alleviate pressure on nerves.

• Trigeminal Neuralgia Balloon Decompression


This procedure uses a balloon to widen a compressed nerve, relieving facial pain.

• Botox Injection for Migraine


Injections of botulinum toxin can help prevent migraines.
